Our Story

Founded in 2020, Chai & Coffee Haven began as a small dream to bring authentic flavors to our community. We source the finest coffee beans and tea leaves from around the world to create memorable experiences.

Our baristas are trained in traditional brewing methods, ensuring every cup is crafted with care and passion. Whether you prefer the boldness of coffee or the comfort of chai, we have something special for you.
